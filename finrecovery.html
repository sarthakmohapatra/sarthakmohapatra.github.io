About Yanu Financial Recovery

Our foundation of industry expertise and decades of experience combines with our approach of empathy and respect for those in debt to deliver maximum financial recovery for our clients. Our commitment to professionalism, industry standards and safeguarding our clients' reputation results in partnerships that last decades. As a collection agency with vast experience, we understand that each debt is unique and by helping those in trouble see solutions we deliver unparalleled results.

We fully understand that debt recovery is a highly specialist subject, involving sensitivity, caution and meticulous care. We fully recognize that we are frequently dealing with sensitive issues while recovering the delinquent accounts. We always adopt a considerate, friendly and courteous approach with the debtors to ensure better business relationship whilst simultaneously protecting the interests of our clients. Our services are tailor made to suit your needs. We aim to work as a team with you to maximize your cash flow and minimize your debtor days. Using our expertise we recommend a plan for debt recovery. This may be a discreet approach where you wish to maintain a longstanding business relationship, or a more aggressive programme for debtors with whom you no longer wish to trade. All kinds of organisations enjoy the benefits of our services. We recover overdue accounts for manufacturers, retailers, exporters, logistics specialists, accountants, insolvency practitioners, individuals, MNC’s, and others. We set high standards and pride ourselves on providing an effective, efficient and ethical service. RBI has over 20 years debt recovery experience. Our core strength is our personal service – providing successful mediation, dispute resolution and installment planning to ensure successful debt recovery.

How we work?

We work on consultation/ professional fee model with success fee ranging from 15% to 45% of the claim amount recovered plus advanced marginal handling charges. that is determined by the following factors, which will be adjusted towards success fee etc.

  • 1. Age of the Commercial debt claim
  • 2. Amount of bad debts due
  • 3. Number of recovery Attorneys/ agencies that have previously worked on the account
  • 4. Location of debtor
  • 5. Whether there is a guarantor of the debt obligation
  • 6. Mode of Recovery
  • 7. Merits of the claim
